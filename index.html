When Kira ends up in Principal Keller's office, she's immediately struck by his firm, caring demeanor. Never in her life has she wanted to please someone as much as she wants to please Principal Keller. As sweet Kira gives herself over, the trust she places in him makes Principal Keller's heart swell. When he looks at her with that gaze of warm approval, everything that could be seen as so very wrong seems so, so right. As they discover each other, they discover lost aspects of themselves.
